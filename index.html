About us

Every year, thousands of government schemes, scholarships, and internships go unused — not because people aren't eligible, but because finding the right one means digging through dozens of scattered websites, notices, and PDFs.

Opportunity Hub was built to close that gap. Tell us about yourself once — your education, income, location, and interests — and we instantly match you with the opportunities you actually qualify for, all verified and kept up to date.

Our mission is simple: make sure no student or citizen misses out on an opportunity they deserve, just because they didn't know it existed.
